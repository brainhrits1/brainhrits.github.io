Job Requirements

Scrum Master

New Jersey, United States

Brain Wave IT Solutions is looking for a talented Scrum master. The Agile Scrum Master is responsible for end-to-end management and delivery of a material change for a project of high complexity across multiple departments within a single business unit. The Scrum Master facilitates the Agile methodology and activities, manages, leads and directs the work efforts of resources that are functionally aligned as part of the project team.

Experience

  • Certified Scrum Master or Certified Scrum Professional (CSM or CSP)
  • Knowledge of and experience in the machine learning/predictive analytics space strongly desired
  • BA/BS or equivalent work experience required
  • 3+ years of experience practicing Scrum and Lean Startup methodologies

Responsibilities

  • Facilitate meetings and discussions
  • Keep artifacts organized, updated and visible
  • Perform ongoing assessments of the teams' Agile maturity and implement iterations as needed
  • Partner with product owners to ensure vision, road-map and personas are well defined
  • Help teams continuously reflect and improve, becoming more effective in their roles and increasing their predictability, collaboration and quality practices
  • Guide teams to understand the "why" behind the practices - sprint events (stand-ups, demos, retrospectives), road-mapping, backlog management (definition, grooming, prioritization)
  • Cultivate communities of practice
  • Embody a servant leadership mindset and learn when to challenge teams and when to get out of the way.
  • Grow your skill-set, learn new tools and techniques, and proactively develop innovative solutions
